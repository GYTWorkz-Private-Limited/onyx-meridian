The Problem

AI exists across the enterprise. But does it truly operate within it?

Copilots remain trapped inside individual applications. Automation runs without business context. Insights stay disconnected from execution.

The result is not intelligence — it is fragmentation. Organizations generate more data than ever, yet struggle to answer simple operational questions in real time.

AI-enabled organizations add intelligence around existing workflows. AI-native organizations build intelligence into the workflows themselves — so context moves across systems, decisions happen in real time, and outcomes remain governed and traceable.

This creates a continuous layer of intelligence connecting knowledge, insight, and action across the enterprise.

The Architecture

Two layers. One enterprise system.

Layer 01

Co-Work

Employee Intelligence Layer

Co-Work brings AI directly into the flow of work. It embeds intelligence inside enterprise applications through Application Use Agents — AI coworkers that understand workflows, system state, and ongoing actions.

Capabilities
  • Understands workflows and system state
  • Assists users with contextual guidance
  • Executes within defined boundaries
  • Learns how the business operates
Layer 02

Digital Twin

Enterprise Intelligence Layer

Digital Twin creates a live, operational model of the enterprise. It captures what is happening now — and what is likely to happen next. Not a dashboard. A model of the enterprise itself.

Capabilities
  • Real-time enterprise awareness
  • Predictive insight and simulation
  • Governed decision-making
  • Continuous state synchronization
